App Won't Launch

  • Verify your device meets iOS requirements
  • Try restarting your device
  • Reinstall the application

Save Data Loss

  • Game data is stored locally and in the cloud
  • Ensure adequate storage space
  • Sign in to sync cloud data

Companion System Issues

  • New players can claim a starter companion on the companion page
  • Companions need regular feeding and interaction
  • Companions gain experience and level up during exploration

GPS Location Issues

  • Ensure location services are enabled for the app
  • GPS works better in open areas
  • Indoor locations may affect accuracy

Performance Tips

  • Close other background applications
  • Ensure battery is adequately charged
  • Keep the app updated to the latest version
